📱 Delete directly in the app Fastest

The quickest way to delete your account is directly from within FlagPost:

  1. 1 Open the FlagPost app and sign in.
  2. 2 Tap the Settings icon (top right).
  3. 3 Scroll to Danger zone.
  4. 4 Tap Delete account and data and confirm.

Your account and all associated data will be deleted immediately.

✉️ Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request deletion by email. Please include your registered nickname in the message so we can identify your account.

Send deletion request

Requests sent by email are processed within 30 days.

🗑️ What data gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile (nickname, flag, travel status, location)
  • All messages posted on the Board
  • All chat conversations and chat history
  • Location data and location history
  • Any blocked-user records associated with your account
